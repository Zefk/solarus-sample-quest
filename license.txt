We give in this file gives the license information of all data files of the
official Solarus sample quest.

Resources from the sample quest are also used by
the Solarus testing quest
(https://github.com/solarus-games/solarus/tree/dev/tests/testing_quest)
and by the Solarus Quest Editor's initial quest
(https://github.com/solarus-games/solarus-quest-editor/tree/dev/assets/initial_quest).

Some resources come from communities like OpenGameArt, and others are original
creations made by us, the Solarus team.
